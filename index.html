Celebrating Creative Excellence

01.

About OVALAY

Celebrating Design

The OVALAY awards program was created to celebrate the exemplary, stand out Creative work of the best and brightest  design professionals from all over Nigeria.

It initially started, back in 2016, as an idea to curate the best of the best work in the form of an annual zeitgeist. Now, more evolved, OVALAY seeks to become the main stay of the Nigerian creative space, recognizing creative work as well as inspiring & encouraging upcomers to push the envelope and think beyond the box.

02.

How it Works

This year, we’ll be accepting submissions and nominations from Individual Creatives & Agencies. that fit into the following categories

  • Branding
  • Photography
  • Visual Arts
  • Responsive Web Designs
  • Web and Mobile Apps
  • Motion Graphics
  • Games
  • Comics
  • Digital Campaigns

If you get nominated?

Of the numerous creative works that will be submitted, only a handful will make to be Nominees (and that’s no small feat).

Nominees also get the chance to become winners of their category.

03.

Submissions

Submissions of Entries by Individual designers & Creative Agencies will kickoff on the 16th of October 2017 and will close on 5th of November 2017.

Submitting as an Individual

Individual Creatives will be allowed a maximum of 2 submissions per category. You can only apply for 3 categories max

Submitting as an Agency?

Individual Creatives will be allowed a maximum of 2 submissions per category. You can only apply for 3 categories max
